Frequently asked questions

How do I play?

A hidden stack of soda cans is waiting on screen — but you can't see the order. Drag cans from the bottom shelf into your stack. Once every slot is filled, the game tells you how many cans are in the right position (but not which ones!). Rearrange and try again until you get a PERFECT MATCH. Turn on 💡 Hints in Settings to also see how many cans are the right brand in the wrong spot.

I bought Remove Ads but I still see ads

Open Settings (the ⚙️ gear, top right) and tap Restore purchase. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you bought it with. If ads still appear after restoring, email us with your purchase receipt and we'll sort it out.

I got a new phone — is my purchase gone?

No! Remove Ads is a one-time purchase tied to your Apple ID. On the new device, open Settings → Restore purchase and it comes right back, free.

What is the Daily puzzle?

Every day there's one puzzle that's the same for every player in the world, with up to 10 tries. Win it to grow your 🔥 streak — and share your emoji result grid with friends. Revealing the answer or running out of tries breaks the streak, so guess carefully!

My streak reset — why?

Streaks grow only when you win the daily puzzle on consecutive calendar days. Missing a day, revealing the answer, or running out of tries resets it. (We know — it stings. That's the game!)

Leaderboards aren't working

Leaderboards use Apple's Game Center. Make sure Game Center is enabled in iOS Settings and you're signed in. Scores submit automatically after each win.

How is my data handled?

The short version: no account is required, your scores live on your device, and our analytics are anonymous. Ads are served by Google AdMob with your consent. Full details in our Privacy Policy.
