Crack the Cans Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 10, 2026

The short version

Crack the Cans needs no account and asks for nothing personal. Your scores, streaks, and settings stay on your device. We use anonymous analytics to improve the game, and ads (which you can remove with a one-time purchase) are served by Google AdMob — with your consent where the law requires it.

What stays on your device

Game settings, best scores, daily streaks, and play statistics are stored locally on your device only. Deleting the app deletes them. We have no servers and no account system.

Analytics (TelemetryDeck)

We use TelemetryDeck to understand how the game is played — for example, how many rounds are won or how often the daily puzzle is completed. TelemetryDeck is designed for privacy: data is anonymized before it reaches their servers, no advertising identifiers are used, and neither we nor TelemetryDeck can identify you from it.

Advertising (Google AdMob)

The free version of Crack the Cans shows banner and full-screen ads served by Google AdMob. All ads are non-personalized: we never request your device's advertising identifier, and the app does not track you across other companies' apps or websites. To serve and measure ads, Google receives basic device information, an approximate location inferred from your IP address, and ad interaction data, as described in Google's privacy & terms.

  • EEA/UK users: a GDPR consent form is shown before any ad is requested, as required by European law.
  • Removing ads: the one-time "Remove Ads" purchase removes all advertising (and AdMob with it) permanently.

Purchases & Game Center

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple; we never see your payment details. Leaderboards use Apple's Game Center under Apple's privacy policy.

Children

Crack the Cans is fun for all ages but is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them.
